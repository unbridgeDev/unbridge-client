Unbridge · split custody · two processes

The server holds a share. It still cannot sign without you.

Your key share lives in this page and never leaves it. A separate signer process holds the other share. To sign, the two co-operate. The signer, given its full share, cannot finalize alone. This is the whole custody model, running as two real processes.
