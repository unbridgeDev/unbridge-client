Bind to your Solana account

Register this wallet's group key to your Solana identity on-chain, so the protocol and any relayer can tie your account to the key your shares sign under. It is an ordinary account you control; the shares behind it are never assembled.

Recovery

Your share lives only in this page. Back it up, encrypted with a passphrase, so losing this device does not lose the wallet. The backup is useless to anyone without your passphrase, and the signer never sees it.

Tip: after backing up, reload the page and Restore to prove the wallet survives a lost device.

What this is. The signer process stands in for the operator network. In production its share is produced by the on-chain operator set. The property does not change: hand over every operator secret and your funds still do not move without the share you hold, on this device or in your encrypted backup.
