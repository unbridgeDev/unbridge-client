# Getting started

Unbridge runs in the browser at [unbridge.dev](https://unbridge.dev). There is nothing to
install and no account to create. You need a Solana wallet (for example Phantom) on
mainnet.

## A personal vault

The simplest case is a 1-of-1 vault: a private balance only you control.

1. Open [unbridge.dev](https://unbridge.dev) and connect your wallet.
2. Your personal vault appears automatically. It is derived from your wallet, so it
   restores on any device once you reconnect.
3. **Deposit** a standard denomination of SOL. The proof runs on your device in about a
   second, then you approve one transaction in your wallet.
4. **Withdraw** to a fresh address. The relayer submits it and pays the fee, so the
   destination is not linked to your wallet.

## A team vault

For a shared treasury with `t`-of-`n` control:

1. One member creates the vault and picks the threshold.
2. Each member joins and runs their part of distributed key generation. When everyone has
   contributed, the vault is ready and has a single on-chain address.
3. Anyone can **deposit** into it.
4. To **send**, a member proposes a withdrawal. It appears in the Transactions tab. Each
   member approves with one click; approval is resumable, so a member can approve and come
   back later. Once the threshold approves, the proposing member executes it.

## Recovering a vault

Your funds are not stored in the browser. A vault rebuilds from on-chain data plus your
wallet:

- **Personal:** reconnect the wallet; the vault and its notes are rescanned from the pool.
- **Team:** use "Sync my vaults", which re-derives your key share from your wallet
  signature and pulls your encrypted shares.

## Good hygiene

- Always withdraw to a **fresh address**. Reusing an address that is already tied to you
  re-links the withdrawal.
- Privacy improves with the size of the pool. Early on, timing can still correlate a
  deposit and a withdrawal; spacing them out helps.
- Read [Security](security.mdx) before depositing meaningful amounts. The protocol is
  unaudited and the setup ceremony is ongoing.
